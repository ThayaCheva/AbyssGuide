The Delvers & Cave Raiders

Delvers, AKA Cave raiders, are a group of adventurers whose roles are to dive into the giant menacing pit called the “Abyss” to explore its many secrets. Delvers are considered to be heros as most of their findings are very significant to the study of the Abyss, in the town of Orth, many children aspire to become a delver as they grow up. The delvers are differentiated by their skills, the White Whistles are the most skilled, followed by Blacks, Blues, Red and Bell.

The White Whistles

The White Whistles are considered to be a second grade artifacts that are created from Life Reverberating Stones called “Your Worth”, the shape of a white whistle is unique to its owner due to their owner’s alteration. Only certain relics that can be activated by a white whistle, making them very valuable. Few divers who are capable of diving past the fifth layer of the abyss can be identified by their possession of a white whistle, there are currently five known and recognized delvers who uses a white whistle, some of them are also thought to have entered the depth of the sixth layer of the abyss.

Bondrewd

Bondrewd The Novel

Bondrewd "The Lord of Dawn" is a Whistle Whistle that has accomplished many discoveries of the abyss. Bondrewd is a scientist, a researcher of the wonders of the abyss; however, he often resort to illegal means for funding for his research. His morals are very controversial as he can resort to developing dangerous drugs and experimenting with live creatures.

Lyza

Lyza The Annihilator

Lyza “The Annihilator” is a White Whistle known for her habits of exterminating any enemies she comes across during her expedition of the abyss. Lyza has been described by many to be a confident woman who enjoys partying, drinking and fighting. She has made several notes and diaries based on the creature of the abyss she has encountered and some recipes of which are edible.

Ozen

Ozen The Immovable

Ozen “The Immovable” is a White Whistle who has successfully retrieved countless numbers of valuable artifacts. Amongst the White Whistle, Ozen is one of the most powerful in terms of physical strength. Ozen is a cold and often cruel person, however, she sometimes can show affection and care for other people as well. She is currently occupied at the Seeker Camp located within the 2nd layer of the abyss.

Srajo

Srajo The Mysterious

Srajo “The Obscure” is a White Whistle, not much information is known about Srajo, not even by his peers. Srajo is assumed to have made great discoveries about the abyss due to his rank. Srajo has lived within the 5th layer for some times like most other White Whistles, After some times, he descended his last dive to the 6th layer of the abyss without informing anyone.

Wakuna

Wakuna The Chosen

Wakuna “The Chosen”, is one of the owner of the White Whistle that is shaped like a whale. Not much is known of him as he has not yet made an appearance, however, it was once stated by Bondrewd that Wakuna is quite old. Before Riko’s adventure, Wakuna has made his last dive to the 6th layer.

Other Classifications

Black Whistles

Those who possesses a black whistle are considered to be adept at delving. They have mastered the technique of diving and ascending and fending themselves from the creatures of the abyss. Black Whistle users are allowed to dive down to the 5th layer of the abyss.

Moon Whistles

Moon Whistles are highly trained and carry substantial amount of knowledge of the abyss, they are positioned to be teachers for lower ranked delvers. They can safely delve down to at most the 4th layer, compared to the Blue Whistles, Moon Whistle users are extensively more capable.

Blue Whistles

Blue Whistles are the adepts. They are more experienced Delvers who have managed to prove themselves. Blue Whistles are allowed to delve down to the 2nd layer of the Abyss, so they most likely possess an acceptable fighting ability to fend off the beasts of the Forest of Temptation.

Red Whistles

Red Whistles are the apprentices. They are rookie Delvers who have a decent knowledge of the Abyss and have descended down to the 1st layer on their own. It is a common rank for prepubescent kids who have studied from an early age.

Bells

Bells are the novices in training, yet to receive a whistle. This rank is given to those who are studying to become Delvers, but have yet to actually go into the Abyss themselves.
