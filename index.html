Made in Abyss (メイドインアビス) is a manga series written by Akihito Tsuki and published by Takeshobo. The manga series was published online in Web Comin Gamma in 2012 and currently have a total of seven volumes. The Made in Abyss manga got an anime adaptation with the same name by studio Kinema Citrus and its first aired from July 7, 2017 to September 29, 2017.

Introduction

Made in Abyss explore the world of the abyss, an expansive and mysterious cave system, the abyss is the only place in the world that isn’t fully explored. No one knows exactly how deep the abyss is, it is inhabited by cryptic and obscure “creatures” and home to many powerful ancient relics whose purpose is still unknown to man. Many generations of adventurers are drawn towards the abyss, overtime, those who are daring enough to challenge and explore the abyss are known as “Delvers”

The story follows an orphan named Riko in a town called Orth, who wishes to become a cave raider and solve the mystery of the abyss. One day, she stumbled upon a little boy named Reg, who later was found to be a robot who ascended from the abyss. Riko and Reg was given an unknown message which was thought to be from Riko’s mother, beginning their adventures to delve the abyss.
