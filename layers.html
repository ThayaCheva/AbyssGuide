The Abyss

The Abyss is an enormous hole consisting of many cave system that was found 1,900 years back. The hole is measured to be over 1,000 meters in diameter and over 20,000 metres deep, but its precise depth is still a mystery. The Abyss has a very special environment containing ancient yet advanced artifacts. Many cave raiders and treasure hunters have confronted the abyss in order to uncover its secrets or just to pursue its valuable treasures.

Curse of the Abyss

Even though the Abyss has many dangerous and hazardous creatures and environment, the greatest challenge that each delver has to face is when ascending from it. This is due to the Curse of the Abyss, it is a sequence of symptoms that occur during an ascension of the abyss with no way of avoiding the effect. The curse mostly manifest after ascending a certain amount and it affects all living being but the creatures that naturally inhabits the Abyss as they have evolved to find ways to counter its effects. The deeper one delves, the more severe the symptoms are, ranging from dizziness and nausea to intense pain throughout the body, loss of senses and even death.

First Layer: Edge of the Abyss

Depth: 0–1,350 meters.

Ascending Strains: Small Dizziness.

Description: The 1st layer is the Abyss ' shallowest area, just below Orth. In the grassy rock faces and petrified trees, the weather is clear and sunny, full of hollows. The ecosystem is made up of mythical creatures, often harmless.

Second Layer: Forest Of Temptation

Depth: 1351-2600 meters.

Ascending Strains: Extreme Nauseousness and headache.

Description: The 2nd layer of the Abyss, The Temptation Forest is the Abyss ' first truly dangerous part. The fauna and climate are shifting abruptly, transforming into a tropical rainforest with immense vegetation. Lower down the layer, there is an area called the “Inverted Forest”, it’s the part the abyss where trees grow upside-down with very strong with, this is where the Observation Camp resides.

Third Layer: The Great Fault

Depth: 2601-7000 meters.

Ascending Strains: Hallucination and Vertigo.

Description: The 3rd layer of the Abyss is a large 4 kilometres cliff consisting of wide area of cave network around the main shaft. The center of the shaft is commonly inhabited by aerial predators such as the Crimson Splitjaws.

Fourth Layer: Goblet of Giants

Depth: 7001-12000 meters.

Ascending Strains: Intense pain and full body bleeding both internally and externally.

Description: The 4th layer of the Abyss, a strange and humid jungle with vines that form a strong concave disc that is capable of gathering surrounding liquids. The Garden of the Flower of Resilience is located 9,000 meters deep within the abyss, it is an area that is brimming with Eternal Fortunes.

Fifth Layer: Sea of Corpse

Depth: 12001-13000 meters.

Ascending Strains: Confusion, Self harm and complete lost of senses.

Description: The 5th layer of the Abyss is the shortest layer depth wise. The Sea of Corpse final layer of the abyss where it is possible to ascend alive, it consist of mostly of large seas and some crystallized areas. At the deepest point of the water lies the Ido front, a large ancient prayer site that is turned into a lab by Bondrewd.
